3 Nights/4 Days - Package 1

Description

Day 1 - Airport to Kandy
 Our representative will be waiting at the arrival lounge to welcome you. Look for your name on the paging boards.Our representative will drive you to Pinnawala elephant orphanage. Pinawala is the largest home for the disables and abundance elephant. After the visit pinnawala, you can be visit herbal and spice gardens. In the evening going to the Temple of the tooth.It is the most secret place of buddhist. Visit to the cultural dance and stay overnight in your hotel.

Day 2 - kandy to nuwara eliya
 Wake up with to the sound of the nature & have a scrumptious breakfast. Kick off the journey to royal botanical garden Peradeniya. Enjoy a nature walk through flora and fauna. Next you start to drive Nuware Eliya, its famous as the Little England. Ramboda waterfall, tea gardens and Gregory lake is the must visit places of Nuwara Eliya. Stay overnight In your hotel at Nuwara Eliya.

Day 3 - Horton Plains
Wake up with misty cold morning your journey will start to Horton Plains. Horton Plains National Park is a protected area in the central highlands of Sri Lanka and is covered by montane grassland and cloud forest. It is rich with biodiversity and endemic species of Sri Lanka. In the afternoon relax at your hotel.

Day 4 - Nuwara Eliya to Airport
Having your hotel breakfast and off to the Bandaranayake International Airport to your flight.
