3 Nights/4 Days - Package 3

Description

Day 1 - Airport to Sigiriya
 Our representative will be waiting at the arrival lounge to welcome you. Look for your name on the paging boards. Our representative will drive you to Sigiriya where the wonders of Sri Lanka rest. Check-in to your hotel and relax. In the evening climb the famous Sigiriya rock fortress which is nearly 200 Meters in height, this will open your eyes about the ancient Sri Lanka architecture where you will see Water gardens, boulder gardens, art galleries & ruins of the castle which was built during antiquity. Stay overnight at Sigiriya in your hotel.

Day 2 - Sigiriya to Kandy
 After breakfast at the hotel start the drive to Kandy.Visit Dambulla,Matal spicy,Herbal garden and Mutta malian kovil. En route visit Dambulla rock temple which displays the ancient stone carving & Painting skills of Sri Lankans. In the evening start to discover Kandy city by visiting Temple of tooth relic which is the most respected religious monument in Sri Lanka which adds glamour to Kandy, Gem museum, Bathik Factory, Lake Drive, Market place & end the day by attending a cultural dance performance. Check-in to the hotel & relax yourself. Overnight stay at Kandy

Day 3 - Kandy to Nuwara eliya
Enjoy your breakfast at the hotel and you will be transferred to Nuwara Eliya the little England. Visit Ella and Royal botanical garden.Because of there invigorating mountain climate and the scenic beauty.Ella in Sri Lanka is a small laid-back town surrounded by the beautiful greens of tea. There are a lot of things to do in Ella. Take one of the most beautiful train rides fromNuwara Eliya to Ella and fall in love with the surroundings. You will probably end up staying longer than you actually planned to. lesure in your hotel at night in Ella. >

Day 4 - Nuwara eliya to airport
